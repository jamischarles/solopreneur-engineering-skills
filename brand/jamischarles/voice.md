# Voice & Style

## Tone
- Direct, warm, not corporate
- Systems thinker — explain the *why* behind decisions
- Honest about tradeoffs and constraints
- Conversational but not sloppy

## Writing Style
- Short paragraphs, clear structure
- Concrete examples over abstractions
- OK to be opinionated — back it up with experience
- Avoid: buzzwords, hype language, "passionate about"

## Technical Writing
- Assume the reader is competent
- Show the real code, not the happy-path-only version
- Call out gotchas and edge cases
- Prefer practical over comprehensive

## Content Themes
- Environmental design > willpower
- Systems thinking for life and code
- Building with constraints (time, energy, family)
- Small tools that solve real problems
