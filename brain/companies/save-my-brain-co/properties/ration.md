# Ration

## Overview
macOS app that rate-limits access to distracting apps and websites — think "time-release" instead of cold-turkey blocking.

## Risk Appetite
**Careful** — native macOS app with auto-update; hard to roll back a bad release.

## Repo
jamischarles/ration

## URL
Not yet on App Store. Distributed via DMG from ration-releases repo.

## Purpose
For developers and knowledge workers who can't stop doomscrolling but find total blockers too rigid. Ration lets you set a daily budget (e.g. 15 min of Twitter) and doles it out in small increments. The core insight: **environmental design beats willpower**.

## Target Audience
- Developers / knowledge workers with attention struggles
- People who've tried Screen Time and found it too easy to bypass
- Night owls caught in late-night scrolling loops

## Positioning
Anti-willpower. "You've tried discipline. You've tried Screen Time." Ration is the structural guardrail that actually works because you can't just tap through it.

## Monetization
Free beta. No paid tier yet. Potential for freemium (free basic blocking, paid for advanced scheduling/analytics).

## Current State
**Pipeline: Launch & Validate > Public Page**
