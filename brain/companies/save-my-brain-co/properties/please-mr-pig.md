# Please Mr Pig

## Overview
iOS app blocker with a judgmental one-eyed pig character who guilts, shames, and coaches you out of opening distracting apps.

## Risk Appetite
**Careful** — iOS app with App Store review cycle; can't hotfix easily.

## Repo
save-your-brain-co/please-mr-pig-ios-app

## URL
Not yet launched. Targeting App Store (iOS 17+, ages 13+).

## Purpose
Same mission as Ration but for iOS, with a radically different approach: a **character-driven gatekeeper**. When you try to open a blocked app, Mr. Pig appears with escalating reactions — from gentle disappointment to full-on rage. Uses Apple's FamilyControls/Screen Time API.

## Target Audience
- iPhone users struggling with phone addiction
- People who respond to humor + light shame as motivation
- Younger audience (teens/young adults) who find standard blockers boring

## Positioning
Character-driven behavioral design. Not another boring settings screen — it's a relationship with a pig who cares about your screen time. The shame/humor angle is the differentiator.

## Monetization
TBD. Likely freemium — free basic blocking, paid for premium pig reactions, custom characters, or advanced scheduling.
