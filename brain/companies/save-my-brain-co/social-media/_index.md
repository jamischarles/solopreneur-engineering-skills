# Save My Brain Co. — Social Media Strategy

## Overview

Social media for Save My Brain properties is founder-story-driven and product-focused. The brand is anti-willpower, pro-environmental-design. Content should feel honest, relatable, and never preachy.

## Global Approach
- savemybrain.co is the hub — social drives traffic there
- Founder story ("built at 3am by a dev who couldn't stop scrolling") as primary narrative
- Show the product in action — screenshots, demos, real usage
- Newsletter as the owned-audience channel

## Property Strategies
- [Ration](ration.md) — primary growth target
- [Reclaim Your Brain](reclaim-your-brain.md) — newsletter / content funnel

## Platforms
- **Twitter/X** — dev community, indie hackers, product announcements
- **Reddit** — r/productivity, r/ADHD, r/macapps for organic reach
- **Product Hunt** — for launches
- **App Store** — SEO and ratings for discovery

## Tone
Direct, warm, self-deprecating. Never corporate. See [voice guide](../../brand/jamischarles/voice.md).
