# Save My Brain Co. — Audience

## Primary Audience

**Developers and knowledge workers who struggle with screen time and attention** — people who've tried willpower-based solutions (Screen Time, cold-turkey blockers, "just don't open it") and found them insufficient.

Common traits:
- Tech-savvy (comfortable with apps, extensions, macOS tools)
- Self-aware about their attention problems but skeptical of simplistic fixes
- Often parents juggling work + family (time is scarce, so wasted screen time hurts more)
- May have ADHD or executive function challenges

## Secondary Audiences
- **Teens / young adults** — for Please Mr Pig specifically (character-driven, humor/shame approach)
- **Anyone with a smartphone addiction** — broader iOS audience for Please Mr Pig

## What They Care About
- Solutions that work structurally, not ones that require discipline
- Tools that respect their intelligence (no patronizing wellness content)
- Authenticity — founder story, real struggles, not corporate marketing
- Small, focused tools that do one thing well

## Where They Hang Out
- Hacker News, Reddit (r/productivity, r/ADHD, r/macapps)
- Twitter/X (dev community, indie hacker community)
- Indie Hackers, Product Hunt (for launches)
- App Store search (for discovery)
