# Husband 101

## Overview
Interactive web app with relationship guidance for husbands — decision trees, best practices, and date strategy playbooks.

## Risk Appetite
**Balanced** — web app in dogfood stage; easy to redeploy.

## Repo
jamischarles/husband-101

## URL
Live (Astro web app, exact URL TBD)

## Purpose
Structured, actionable relationship guidance for married men. Covers emotional intimacy, date planning, communication strategies. Fills a gap where most relationship content is either too generic or not action-oriented.

## Target Audience
- Married men looking to improve their relationship
- Husbands who prefer systems/frameworks over vague advice
- Couples looking for practical date ideas and communication tools

## Positioning
Niche but unique — relationship coaching specifically for husbands, with a systems-thinking approach. Decision trees and playbooks feel natural for the developer/engineer audience that overlaps with Save My Brain's core users.

## Monetization
Currently free. Potential for premium content (deeper playbooks, personalized recommendations).

## Current State
**Pipeline: Build > Dogfood**

Core features working — decision tree navigator, best practices library, date strategy playbook. Being used personally to find rough edges before broader launch.
