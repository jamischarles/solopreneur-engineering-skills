# Uplevel Co. — Social Media Strategy

## Overview

Social media for Uplevel properties should be **automated, high-signal, and low-maintenance**. Each property targets a niche audience. Content is AI-assisted with human editorial oversight.

## Operating Model
- AI generates drafts, summaries, and social posts
- Human reviews and approves before publishing
- Automate scheduling and distribution as much as possible
- Optimize for value-per-post, not posting frequency

## Property Strategies
- [Pierre Notabot](pierre-notabot.md) — SEO-driven, character-powered AI education
- [Husband 101](husband-101.md) — niche relationship content for married men

## Platforms
- **SEO / organic search** — primary discovery channel for all properties
- **Reddit** — niche subreddits per property (targeted, not spammy)
- **YouTube** — potential for short explainers and recaps (especially LDS Conference Recap)
- **Twitter/X** — AI community for Pierre Notabot

## Tone
Varies by property — Pierre has character voice, Husband 101 is direct and systems-oriented, Parent 101 is warm and practical, LDS Conference Recap is reverent and thoughtful. All share a commitment to signal over noise.
