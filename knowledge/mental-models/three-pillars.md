# Three Pillars of a Business

## In a Nutshell

Every business needs three things working: a Product, Lead Generation, and Sales. If any one of the three is missing, you're wasting your time polishing the others. Stop and invest in the one you're avoiding.

## How It Works

The three pillars are:

1. **Product** — something worth buying
2. **Lead Generation** — a way to get it in front of people
3. **Sales** — a way to convert interest into revenue

These three form a chain. Break any link and the whole thing stalls:

- Great product but no leads → nobody knows you exist
- Leads coming in but no sales process → wasted attention, no revenue
- Strong sales but weak product → churn, refunds, bad reputation

The insight isn't just that you need all three. It's that most people instinctively over-invest in the pillar they're comfortable with and avoid the one they're not. The builder keeps perfecting the product. The marketer keeps generating leads. Neither one closes the loop.

The framework forces an honest audit: which pillar am I avoiding?

## When to Use

- When your business feels stuck and you can't figure out why
- When you're spending all your time on one area but not seeing results
- When planning what to work on next — check which pillar is weakest
- As a gut-check before going deep on any single initiative
