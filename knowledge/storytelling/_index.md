# Storytelling

Narrative frameworks for turning ideas into stories people remember, repeat, and act on.
Story isn't decoration — it's the delivery mechanism that carries a message past the audience's defenses and into their long-term memory. These notes extract reusable principles from the best storytellers and storytelling teachers, so I can apply them to products, posts, pitches, and presentations.
