## Guiding Constraints

> These derive from the [manifesto](../brand/jamischarles/manifesto.md) — the fisherman's life, not the businessman's.

- Father of five — time is the scarcest resource
- Optimize for momentum over perfection
- [Tiny bets](../knowledge/mental-models/tiny-bets.md) over big launches — many small experiments, let winners emerge
- [Minimum Effective Dose](../knowledge/principles/minimum-effective-dose.md) — smallest input that produces the desired output; anything beyond is waste
- [Investment Tiers](../knowledge/systems/investment-tiers.md) — Explore (MED) → Double Down (signal-proven) → Craft (Ugmonk-level TLC, max 2)
- Spend time on what actually moves the needle and helps you stand out — not on gold-plating things nobody's asked for
- Ship something small, learn, iterate
