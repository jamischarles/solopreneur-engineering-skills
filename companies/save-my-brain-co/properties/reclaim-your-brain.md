# Reclaim Your Brain (savemybrain.co)

## Overview
Marketing site and brand hub for the Save My Brain product suite. Newsletter signup funnel and download portal.

## Risk Appetite
**Balanced** — public marketing site, but easy to redeploy on Vercel.

## Repo
jamischarles/save-my-brain-co-www

## URL
savemybrain.co (hosted on Vercel)

## Purpose
Unified brand presence for all attention/focus products. Tells the founder story, showcases Ration (and upcoming products), and captures newsletter signups via Resend.

## Target Audience
Same as Ration / Please Mr Pig — people frustrated with their screen time who've tried willpower-based solutions and failed.

## Positioning
"You've tried discipline. You've tried Screen Time. You've tried just not opening it." Founder-story-driven, anti-willpower messaging. Authentic voice (includes self-deprecating mom quotes as social proof).

## Monetization
Newsletter signups as the primary growth lever. No direct revenue — it's a funnel to the apps.

## Current State
**Pipeline: Build > Idea**

Concept stage for reclaimyourbrain.app. Problem and audience being defined. (Note: savemybrain.co is live separately as the brand hub for the Save My Brain suite.)

## Growth / Marketing
- SEO for "app blocker," "screen time alternative," "phone addiction" keywords
- Newsletter as owned audience channel
- Founder story content (blog posts, social)
- Cross-links to all properties
