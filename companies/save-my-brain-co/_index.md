# Save My Brain Co.

## Mission

Reclaim attention in a distracted age. We build tools that use **environmental design over willpower** to help people take back their time and focus.

## Philosophy

You've tried discipline. You've tried Screen Time. You've tried just not opening it. None of it worked — because the problem isn't you, it's the environment. Save My Brain builds structural guardrails that make the right choice the easy choice.
