# Uplevel Co. — Audience

## Primary Audience

**People who want to use AI to genuinely improve their lives** — not for shortcuts or laziness, but to educate themselves, build good habits, and develop meaningful skills.

Common traits:
- Curious about AI but want practical, life-improving applications
- Value self-improvement — as partners, parents, learners, and in their faith
- Prefer structured, actionable content over vague advice
- Busy (often parents, professionals) — need high-signal, low-noise content

## Per-Property Audiences

- **Pierre Notabot** — Developers and AI-curious people learning LLM concepts (beginner to pro)
- **Husband 101** — Married men who prefer systems/frameworks over generic relationship advice
- **Parent 101** — Parents looking for practical, evidence-based parenting guidance
- **LDS Conference Recap** — Latter-day Saints who want to deeply engage with conference talks and spiritual content

## What They Care About
- Content that respects their time and intelligence
- Practical takeaways they can act on immediately
- Trustworthy, well-curated information (not AI slop)
- Depth when they want it, summaries when they don't

## Where They Hang Out
- Reddit (r/parenting, r/marriage, r/latterdaysaints, r/LocalLLaMA)
- Twitter/X (AI community, dev community)
- YouTube (educational content, conference talk discussions)
- LDS-specific forums and social groups
